About BIMEX

we make it simple but significant.

We are a diverse group of individuals, brought together by technological innovations and inspired by the need to share these with the world. We are driven by an immense passion for futuristic technology in the Architecture, Engineering and Construction industry. We have specialized in Building Information Modeling, where our scope covers BIM training and consultancy.

Our Services

BIM Training

We have simplified training courses, aimed at students that are seeking a career in the Architecture, Engineering and Construction industry, to seasoned professionals who wish to increase their knowledge on BIM. Our training is geared at imparting students with both fundamental and advanced BIM skill sets, and also keeping them up to date with the latest trends such as Computational Design & Automation.

BIM Consultancy

We offer BIM consultancy services to companies wishing to fully transition into BIM platforms. These range from BIM Project consultancy, conversion of 2D CAD drawings to 3D models, 3D visualization, creation of workshop, fabrication and As-built drawings, and 3D model walk-throughs. We aim to meet the ever increasing client needs, such as employing the use of virtual reality to enhance project experience.

BIM Implementation

Many ask the question why BIM is necessary when the old modes of project execution are still used. Shifting to BIM enhances your workflow and quality of project delivery, and has a positive impact on time and cost on executing tasks. We help you understand BIM by employing the use of customized solutions to improve workflows, whether you are a developer, consultant or contractor.
