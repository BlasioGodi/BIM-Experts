Generation of workshop, prefabricated &
As-built drawings

We carefully generate BIM Construction models for Contractors. These can be used to extract workshop and prefabricated drawings for site installations. The model's accuracy guarantees consistency whereby any update on the model is automatically captured in the drawings.

3D BIM Coordination, Visualization
& Clash Detection

We provide consultancy on BIM models that require multi-disciplinary coordination and clash-detection, enabling all disciplines to collaborate and iron out issues that may arise during the different stages of the project lifecycle.

2D-3D CAD to BIM
Conversion

We facilitate the conversion of 2D CAD projects into 3D BIM models, for those looking to move onto BIM platforms but lack the expertise on how to go about it. Our BIM conversion and modeling processes are aligned towards meeting the requirements of the PAS 1192 framework.

Generation of detailed Bills of
Materials for Cost Estimation

The extraction of accurate Bills of materials is made possible through the use of BIM models. When modeled accurately, the information extracted can then be used for project cost estimation. BIM model data has proven useful and guarantees cost savings of upto 20-25% for those on the platform. We guide building professionals on how to achieve these cost savings.

Sprinkler Hydraulic
Calculations

We have invested ample time in developing calculation processes that can be used on BIM models. We have specialized in shortening the time taken to conduct lengthy and repetitive tasks, such as Sprinkler Hydraulic calculations, by employing the use of Computational design practises on our BIM models. All calculations conducted are as per NFPA 13 and BS EN 12845 Standards.
